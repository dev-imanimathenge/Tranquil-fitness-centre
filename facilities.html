Our Facilities

Everything you need for a complete wellness experience all under one roof.

State-of-the-Art Gym

Our spacious gym is equipped with the latest cardio machines, free weights, and strength training equipment to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Serene Yoga Studio

Our tranquil yoga studio offers a peaceful environment for practicing yoga, meditation, and mindfulness. With natural light and calming decor, it's the perfect space to find your inner balance.

Running Track

An indoor running track with shock-absorbent flooring, perfect for high-intensity interval training.

Relaxation Suite

Relax before and after your workout in our comfortable relaxation suite.

Steam and Sauna Room

Detoxify and relax in our state of the art steam room and traditional sauna.

Hot Tub

Relax and unwind in our jacuzzi-style hot tub, perfect for post-workout recovery.

Cold and Hot Dip

Experience the contrast of our cold and hot dip, perfect for muscle recovery and circulation.

Restaurant

Refuel after your workout at our on-site restaurant, offering healthy and delicious options.

Golf Simulator

Experience a top of the line golf simulator that feels like you are on the course with realistic courses and instant shot analysis.

Indoor Basketball Court

Enjoy a game of basketball in our indoor court, perfect for pick-up games or organized tournaments.

Heated Swimming Pool

Temperature controlled pool for year-round swimming comfort.

Indoor Climbing Wall

Challenge yourself on our indoor climbing wall, suitable for all skill levels.

Spin Studio

High-energy spin classes led by experienced instructors in a motivating environment.

Boxing Ring

Train like a pro in our fully equipped boxing ring, perfect for sparring and training sessions.

Wellness Spa

Indulge in a variety of spa treatments designed to relax and rejuvenate your body and mind.

Padel Court

Enjoy a game of padel in our modern court, equipped with the latest technology for an optimal playing experience.
